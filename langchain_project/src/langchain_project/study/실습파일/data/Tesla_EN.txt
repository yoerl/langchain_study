Tesla, Inc., headquartered in Austin, Texas, is a leading American electric vehicle manufacturer. Founded in 2003 by Martin Eberhard (CEO) and Marc Tarpenning (CFO), Tesla saw significant involvement from Elon Musk in 2004. Musk, co-founder of PayPal and Zip2, became the largest shareholder and chairman, steering the company towards its current success. The company is named after the renowned physicist and electrical engineer Nikola Tesla. Tesla went public on NASDAQ in June 2010.

In 2023, Tesla sold 1,808,581 vehicles, marking a 37.65% increase from 2022. From 2012 to the third quarter of 2023, Tesla's cumulative global sales exceeded 4,962,975 vehicles. According to SMT Packaging, Tesla's 2023 sales accounted for approximately 12.9% of the global electric vehicle market.
